About Me

I'm a proud Nashville native, who's been so lucky to have had amazing opportunities to learn, live, and teach in some amazing places. Attending Belmont University, I studied abroad in both Greece and London. After graduation, I earned a teaching certificate and was hired by the Japanese government to teach English to primary and high school students in rural Japan.

I found my wife while in Japan, and we moved back to Nashville so I could help father, a homebuilder, capitalize on the booming housing market. I gained my builder's license and my real estate license in the years after. I earned a postgraduate certificate in international construction management from the University of Bath in the United Kingdom via distance learning.

Currently, I am retraining to become a web developer as a member of the first cohort of Vanderbilt University's Coding Bootcamp.

I live in West Nashville with my wife, twin girls, Mia and Juliette, and our fur baby, Roux. We are huge nerds! Interests include: Star Trek, Battlestar Galactica, Doctor Who, Japanese culture, anime, and Ancient Greek history and culture.
